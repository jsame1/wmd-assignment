Security systems are designed to protect individuals, properties, and assets from potential threats and unauthorized access. They can include a variety of components such as alarms, surveillance cameras, access control systems, and security personnel. The goal of a security system is to deter criminal activity, detect intrusions, and provide a response mechanism in case of emergencies. Effective security systems often integrate technology with human oversight to ensure comprehensive protection.
